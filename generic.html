About Me

Hi, I'm Ty Chen, a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo.
I have a passion for 3D modeling whether it being in Blender or SolidWorks.
I enjoy turning ideas into tangible results!

I'm currently pursuing my degree in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Waterloo, where I’ve completed my first co-op term in the winter at Bend All Automotive which involved testing automotive fluid lines in a lab envoirnment. I have done a highschool co-op at Greentec aswell as a full-time summer job, which involved sustainable technology recycling. These roles have helped me build a solid foundation in design, manufacturing, and technical communication.

Outside of academics, I spend time developing personal projects in Blender, where I’ve explored everything from isometric interiors to stylized weapons and low-poly environments. I enjoy going to the gym and also rock climbing, mainly bouldering (currently at V5). Now I spend most of my free time making music in FL studios, which allows my cretivity to flow. I also enjoy martial arts—I’ve earned a Second Degree Black Belt recently aswell as an International First Degree Black Belt in Meibukan Goju-Ryu Karate. I have recently learned how to code and sometimes take part in coding challenges whenever I can!
