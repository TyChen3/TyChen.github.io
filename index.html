Low-Poly Mitsubishi
Lancer Evolution X

For my Grade 12 final project, I designed and modeled a low-poly Mitsubishi Lancer EVO X using Blender.
The project focuses on relatively clean topology and optimized geometry. Despite the simplified form,
the model retains the stylistic features of the EVO X, including the aggressive bumpers, hood, spoiler, and overall shape.
This model showcases my skills in Blender and my ability to accurately follow reference photos and blueprints.
